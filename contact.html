Contact

I work with organisations facing imperfect data and limited resources. If you’d like to discuss a project, collaboration, or partnership, I am happy to start a conversation.

The best way to contact me is to send a message using the form below.

Alternatively, you can email me. Please include a short description of your project including context, data sources, and constraints, and what decision you’re trying to make.

I’m happy to communicate in English and Mandarin, and can also hold conversations in Cantonese, German, and basic Russian.

I will only use your details to respond to your enquiry.
