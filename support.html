Support

I am an independent data and Hybrid Intelligence advisor with a background in applied data science, machine learning, and telecommunications. My work bridges rigorous data science, machine learning, and decision systems design - emphasising transparency, reliability, and real-world impact. I support organisations in building lightweight analytics, reproducible workflows, and accountable machine learning tools that perform under uncertain and messy data conditions.

Hardware & Infrastructure

Support is used to maintain and upgrade essential hardware, including development laptops with GPU capability, local compute resources, and network-attached storage (NAS) for secure data handling, backups, and reproducible workflows.

Software & Subscriptions

Ongoing subscriptions support day-to-day delivery and collaboration, including professional tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud, ChatGPT, Google Workspace, and Squarespace for design, automation, communication, and web presence.

Office & Working Environment

Support contributes to necessary office-related costs, including utilities and bills, workspace rental when required, and essential equipment such as desks, chairs, and ergonomic setups to sustain focused and effective work.

Travel & Client Engagement

Travel and client-facing expenses are supported where appropriate, including transport, accommodation, and modest client entertainment. These activities enable collaboration, relationship-building, and effective delivery across locations.

What support is not used for

Support is not used for personal expenses unrelated to work delivery, speculative investment activity, or non-essential overhead. All support is directed toward activities that directly enable research, development, collaboration, and accountable delivery.

Funding philosophy. Support is used in a practical, accountable manner, with minimal overhead and a clear link between resources, delivery, and outcomes.

Reporting & accountability. Use of support can be documented and reported where required, with spending and outcomes aligned to agreed objectives.

Institutions, funders, and partners interested in supporting this work - through collaboration, sponsorship, or grant funding - are welcome to get in touch via the contact page
